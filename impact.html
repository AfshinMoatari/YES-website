  • CHILD SPONSORSHIP

    As many as 115 million children, of primary school age, are not enrolled in school. In most developing countries, public school is not free. The costs of books, uniforms, and teachers’ salaries are borne by the students’ families. With your help, we can pay for these costs and make sure they are enrolled into school so that we can break the cycle of poverty and illiteracy.
    Your service fee for us goes towards sponsoring one marginalized school aged child for a month, per 60 contractual hours, ensuring all necessities are taken care of. By using our service, we help sponsor:

    Tution

    Book & Stationary

    Uniform

    Health Checkups

    Nutritious Food

    Love & Care

    Moni is an adorable girl who lives with her parents in Gaibandha. Moni’s father is a day laborer and her mother is a housewife. Her father has to work really hard to run his small family of thrree, but his income is not enough to be able to afford Moni’s educational expenses.

    Jonardhon lives with her parents in Dinakpur. Jonardhon dreams of one day becoming a police officer, but his father is a mere construction worker whereas his mother is a housewife. Because of this, his family cannot afford the right education to help Jonardhon fulfill his dream.

    As a social business one of the fundamental rules that we follow is that we are not profit driven. Therefore, all our profit goes to support social causes.

    We strive to understand your business’ values so that we can choose the social development projects that have the best fit with your values. For instance, if you are a business that advise other business’ on how to implement technological strategies, then we will find projects that has development of technological innovations that support society as its core purpose. We will always provide you with at least 3 options to choose from and we will make sure that the projects supply you with content from your contributions, so that you can strengthen your CSR portfolio. The key areas that we currently have projects from are:

    Healthy Living

    Self-Sufficiency

    Social Inclusion
